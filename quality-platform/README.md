# Quality Dashboard

Quality Dashboard is the internal quality operations app used to manage tutor reviews, flags, weekly reports, objections, and reviewer analytics.

## Main roles

- `Admin`: manages tutors, reviewers, cycles, reports, flags, and analytics.
- `Reviewer`: completes assigned quality reviews for tutors.
- `Tutor`: submits session or student issues, views weekly reports, and objects to flags.
- `Team Lead`: monitors mentor, tutor, and reviewer quality performance for mapped mentors.

## Weekly flow

1. Admin prepares tutor data, reviewer accounts, mappings, and cycle dates.
2. Tutors submit session or student issues when needed.
3. Reviewers complete their assigned reviews.
4. Admin checks reports, handles flag decisions and objections, and publishes reports.
5. Tutors view published reports and follow up on flags.
6. Team leads monitor quality trends for their teams.
